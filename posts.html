Thoughts and Musings

Here you can find all my published posts. I write about my experiences in the course of building and running my startups. You can also find some poems that I occasionally pen down.

ಅಂತ್ಯವಿಲ್ಲದ ಪ್ರಯಾಣ
Anthyavillada Prayana in Kannada stands for journey with no End. After my mothers demise I was inconsolable for a period of time. I had to think of expressing my feelings in some way and this was the poem I wrote about my relationship with my mother, especially in the last few months of her life.

ಹುಡುಕಿದಳು ಆತ್ಮ ಸಂಗಾತಿ
Hudukidalu Aathma Sangaathi in Kannada means woman searching for her soul mate. I dedicate this poem to all women who found their love, the soul mate of their life. Life is not just a series of problems, life does change for good!

ಸಾಧಕಿಯ ಮನಸ್ಸು
Saadhakiya Manassu in Kannada stands for mindset of a woman achiever. This poem is an attempt at decoding the mindset of women who battle everyday in the hopes of achieving something big one day.
